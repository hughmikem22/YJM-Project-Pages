PROGRAMS

Every year YOUTH JUSTICE MILWAUKEE reaches out to schools and street corners for teens who are down to make a change in themselves and the city.
Our current members and alum will tell you that joining YOUTH JUSTICE MILWAUKEE was one of the best decisions they ever made.

If you are:

-Tired of the violence in your school, home, or neighborhood

-Impacted by having a parent or loved one in prison

-Down to meet and connect with new people from different schools

-Tired of being bored after school

-Looking to learn new things about yourself and the world around you

-Between the ages of 13-18

-Ready to make a difference

YOUR FUTURE IS HERE

When you join YOUTH JUSTICE MILWAUKEE you are joining a family of young leaders and supportive adults
committed to helping you make a difference in yourself and the community.
As a member you must be willing to participate in at least two activities per week.
These activities include leadership workshops, movie nights, academic support, life-skills sessions, brother to brother, sister to sister, technology classes, and more.

If you are interested in joining you must take the following next steps:

STEP 1: Complete your application.

STEP 2: Tell a friend or classmate to complete an application

STEP 3: Attend one of the mandatory information-sessions
